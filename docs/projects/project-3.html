AI Driven Threat Modeling

Al-driven, STRIDE-based threat modeling tool designed to automate and enhance the cybersecurity threat modeling process.

Overview

This project presents an Al-driven, STRIDE-based threat modeling tool designed to automate and enhance the cybersecurity threat modeling process. The system leverages Large Language Models with multimodal capabilities to analyze textual documentation, visual diagrams, and source code, producing comprehensive and consistent threat assessments. By separating discovery from analysis, the pipeline ensures systematic, breadth-first threat identification followed by in-depth per-threat enrichment, including risk scoring, CWE/CAPEC mapping, and mitigation planning. The result is a repeatable, scalable, and integration-ready workflow that delivers prioritized, testable mitigations and enables security teams to understand and nore efficiently.
