About This Course

This is the first lab course for students enrolled in the Cybersecurity concentration of the Professional Master's Program. The course covers three core topics: penetration testing, web security, and cloud security.

As part of the course, studnets enrolled collaborate in groups of 3-4 to conduct thorough research and implement state-of-the-art methodologies to solve the most pressing security challenges.

This year 8 groups of talented students worked towards developing solutions to enhance cybersecurity practices in this age of AI boom

This portfolio is a brief introduction to all the projects and the talented students who developed these solutions.

Each student's LinkedIn profile is available on the project page in case you would like to reach out to them, or simply know them further!
